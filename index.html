About Us

RGNICT offers a diverse portfolio of career-focused programs including O Level, CCC, ADCA, DCAA, DOAP, BCA, MCA, PGDCA, BA, MA — and many more. Located in Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh, RGNICT is known for delivering high-quality education with a strong emphasis on practical skills and industry-aligned curriculum.

Whether you're starting your journey in IT or advancing your academic path, our programs are designed to prepare you for in-demand roles such as software developer, database administrator, system analyst, IT consultant, and beyond.

At RGNICT, you'll be part of an innovative academic environment that supports your growth, fosters creativity, and equips you for long-term success in the digital world.

Dear Students,

Welcome to a new chapter of growth, discovery, and transformation.

In this digital era, technology is no longer just a subject—it is the language of the future. As the Director of this institution, I am proud to say that you are part of a learning community that doesn't just teach computers; we shape creators, innovators, and future leaders of the tech-driven world.

Each course you undertake—whether it’s programming, web development, networking, or data science—is more than just curriculum. It’s a gateway to opportunities, a tool to solve real-world problems, and a way to empower yourself and others.

At our institution, we believe that education is not about memorizing code, but about creating change. We focus on hands-on learning, project-based training, and industry-relevant skills so that every student can leave not only with a certificate, but with confidence and capability.

I urge you to dream big, stay curious, and never stop learning. Technology will challenge you, but it will also reward your efforts with innovation, creativity, and endless possibilities. Don't just aim to get a job—aim to build something, to solve something, to lead something.

Remember, great coders were once beginners. Great engineers were once students like you, sitting in a classroom, learning one line of code at a time. The only difference is—they never gave up.

Let this institution be the launchpad of your journey. Together, let's code the future.

Warm regards,
Mr. Lal Bihari
Director, RGNICT
